Financing and Payment Policies

La Belle Poutine accepts cash, debit cards, credit cards, and mobile payment options. Regular restaurant orders must be paid at the time of purchase.

  • Catering orders may require a deposit before the event date.
  • Large business orders can be paid by invoice if approved by management.
  • Gift cards can be used for dine-in and takeout orders.

Delivery Policies

Delivery is available through local delivery services and selected direct catering orders. Food is packed carefully so it arrives warm and ready to eat.

  • Delivery times may change depending on distance, traffic, and order size.
  • Customers should check their order when it arrives.
  • Large catering deliveries should be ordered in advance.

Return and Refund Policies

Because our products are food items, returns are limited. If there is a mistake with an order, customers should contact the restaurant as soon as possible.

  • Wrong or missing items may be replaced after review.
  • Refunds may be offered if the problem cannot be fixed.
  • Customers should keep their receipt for order problems.
