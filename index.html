Company History

La Belle Poutine is a fictional restaurant based in Montreal. The business was started in 2021 by Marc Gagnon, who wanted to create a simple restaurant focused on fresh fries, warm gravy, and quality Canadian cheese curds. In the beginning, the restaurant only served classic poutine and soft drinks. Over time, the menu grew to include smoked meat poutine, chicken poutine, vegetarian options, burgers, and combo meals.

The restaurant has grown because it focuses on friendly service, fair prices, and food that feels familiar. La Belle Poutine now serves students, families, workers, and visitors looking for a quick Canadian meal.

Why Choose Us?

Customers choose La Belle Poutine because our food is simple, fresh, and filling. We prepare fries daily, use rich gravy, and offer different menu choices for different tastes. Our restaurant also keeps prices reasonable so customers can enjoy a good meal without spending too much.

  • Freshly prepared poutine and sides
  • Fast service for dine-in, takeout, and delivery
  • Friendly employees who care about customers
  • Affordable prices for students and families
  • Community support through local donations

Connection to Sustainable Development Goals

La Belle Poutine connects to community and sustainability goals by reducing waste, using recyclable packaging when possible, and supporting local programs. For every meal sold, the restaurant donates $0.25 to a local homeless shelter. This helps connect the business to the goal of reducing poverty and supporting people in the community.

The restaurant also tries to buy ingredients from Canadian suppliers when possible. This helps local businesses and reduces the distance some food products must travel.
